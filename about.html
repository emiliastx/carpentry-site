TOM DE ST CROIX

About

My name is Tom de St Croix, a cabinet maker from Lewes, East Sussex. I make individual and unique fitted furniture, storage solutions and cabinets.
My work combines traditional joinery and cabinet making with the best modern fittings. All materials are sustainably sourced and I use finishes that do as little harm to the environment as possible, while still being durable.
